=== Note on service round-robin ===
The kessel CLI uses single-Client::connect, not ClusterClient, so a
round-robin via the ClusterIP svc may land a write on a backup which
answers OpResult::Unavailable. ClusterClient is the failover-aware
surface (see crates/kessel-client/src/lib.rs::ClusterClient and the
test cluster::tests::cluster_client_finds_primary_and_is_exactly_once).
Direct-to-primary CRUD above proves the cluster is committing.
